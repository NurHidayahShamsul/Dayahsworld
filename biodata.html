About Me



Howdy!

My name is Nur Hidayah, but everyone calls me Dayah. I've been referred to as "Dayah Kite" by friends and people around me. This because Dayah is the short form for Hidayah meanwhile,  Kite is my father's nickname and is pronounced as "kee-tur"

I'm a 20-year-old student and I am a Kajang girl, born & raised.
I was born on November 6th of 2001, in a hospital in the city of
Kajang called KPMC. I am still living with my parents, which I am quite
grateful for. I wish to become a teacher because I enjoy teaching people,
especially children, and I find it to be a joyful experience whenever I
am able to share my knowledge with others.

Hobby & Interest
Working out is something I enjoy doing. I'll go for a morning jog three times a week, and on weekends, I'll go hiking with my mother. When its pouring outside, I would either work out in my room or go to the local gym, which is about 5-minute drive from my house. Exercising for me is not only about achieving a healthy lifestyle, but it also provides me with mental serenity and may be quite beneficial when I am stressed.

Cafe/Food Hunting

Other than working out, I have a thing for cafe/food hunting. I enjoy visiting the many aesthetic cafés in and around Kuala Lumpur and Selangor with my family and friends. If I have the opportunity, I will at the very least go cafe/food hunting once a month. Every time I go cafe/food hunting, I would also dress up and take tons of pictures since I enjoy dressing up as it gives me confidence. I also desire to open up a cafe of my own one day!
