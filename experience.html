6 MONTHS

While waiting for my SPM results, I decided to work as a part-time at Nike IOI City Mall Putrajaya. I had a tough time finding job at the time because many stores were either already full or did not pay a decent wage. I was fortunate enough that Nike called me for an interview after two months of looking and submitting my résumé to a number of other places and I was hired on the spot. I worked there for six months and gained much knowledge, as well as making many new friends. It was an absolutely great experience to be a part of that team.



3 MONTHS

In July of 2020, I was having a three-month sem break from uni. I made the decision to seek for a part-time work once more in order to fill up my spare time while also earning some money. I went for an interview at Boat Noodle Metropoint, which is a neighbourhood shopping centre near my house. I was hired and worked there for three months. It was challenging  and exhausting since it was my first time working in the F&B sector, and I was responsible for not only preparing the dishes, but also serving, cleaning, and attend customers. In any case, I still enjoy working there and I gained a lot of useful skills and experiences.










ON GOING

On February 2021, I was on my semester break again and was looking for a way to earn my income. I chose to seek for a job at IOI City Mall in Putrajaya. A retail company called Cotton On Kids, which sell children clothing, called me for an interview, and I agreed. I accepted the offer and chose to continue working there till now because all of my classes are now being held online due to the pandemic. Being in my final year of diploma studies, I am only working on weekends and public holidays, which would make things quite chaotic for me if I were to work everyday. I personally believe that this job is my favourite thus far because I previously had retail experience, and I enjoy dealing with customers who are parents, as well as seeing children in and around the stores brings me happiness.
